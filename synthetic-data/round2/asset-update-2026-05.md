## Migration-assessment kit v2 (supersedes v1)

The migration-assessment kit has a **new v2** at <path>. It adds a peak-load sizing worksheet for the
SAP order-integration — added after we under-scoped seasonal load on two retail engagements and hit
rework close to go-live. **Use v2; the v1 path is retired.**

## New rule of thumb baked into v2

Size the SAP order-integration for **peak seasonal load (Black-Friday/holiday)** at scoping, not
steady-state. This is the single most common estimation miss on retail commerce work.
